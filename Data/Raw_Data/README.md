## The **Isolated_Raw_Data** sub-folder contains selected data from **Raw_Data_Sources** Excel workbooks. Each sheet contains isloated data for the project saved in csv format.

### The four Gen_Elect_Results sheets (_Gen_Elect_Results_2008_csv_, _Gen_Elect_Results_2012_csv_, _Gen_Elect_Results_2016_csv_, and _Gen_Elect_Results_2020_csv_)  were isolated from the "Pres General Results" sheets of their respective election year workbooks

### The _2000_appro_table_csv_ and _2010_appro_table_csv_ were isolated from the "apportionment-2020-tableC2.xlsx" sheet displaying apportionment results from 1910 to 2020.
